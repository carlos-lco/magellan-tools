Stars Coordinates

Description - Queries SIMBAD to retrieve coordinates and proper motions, and prints catalog lines formatted for Magellan instruments. Rotation Angle/Mode must be edited a posteriori per observer needs.

Usage:

> magellan_stars_coords std_name

Examples:

> magellan_stars_coords LTT4364
> magellan_stars_coords "SN 1987A"

Dependencies - astroquery (SIMBAD)
